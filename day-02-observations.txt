1.Number of rows:50

2.Number of columns:11

3.Meaning of each column:
Order ID - Unique ID for each order
Date - Date of sales for each order
City - City where sales happened 
State - State of that city
Product - The name of the item that was sold
Category - Type of the product
Salesperson - Person who made the sale
Units - Number of items sold
UnitPrice - Price of one single item
Revenue - Total money earned from one order

4.Observations(One thing that surprised):
Same product have high sales volume but lower total revenue, which shows that price plays an important role in sales
